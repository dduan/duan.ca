This is a pure Web3 app that converts an ERC20 asset in your web3 wallet to a liquidity pool token which is composed of that asset and another.

Simply put in address for the LP token such as this one, pick one asset out of the pair, pick the percent of asset to spend, and press the big button. You'll have to approve two transations, one for swapping part of the asset to the other one in the liquidity pool, then another for providing liquidity. You'll receive the Sushi LP token in your wallet at the end.

As close to 100% as possible of the input asset will become the LP.

Network and Sushi exchange fees apply. This app does not incur any additional fees.

You can stop the transactions, or view them on scanners via Metamask.

This app is only tested on Firefox 89 and Metamask. All code except web3.js is embbeded within this HTML file.
